What is the parking offer?

To prepare your visit to the venue during Rugby World Cup 2023, parking spaces are available according to your needs and your means of transport (cars or motorcycle).

All Rugby World Cup 2023 venues are also accessible to people with disabilities. There are parking spaces dedicated to our accessibility tickets holders.



CARS (light vehicles)

From €15

Maximum 2 parking spaces per match*

All light vehicles
(max. 5m length - 2.30m width - 1.9m height)

Available in Bordeaux, Lille, Lyon, Nantes, Nice

*Subject to availability


ACCESSIBILITY PARKING (cars only)

Free
(reservation required)

Maximum 1 parking space per match*

Only for accessibility tickets holders

Available on all venues

*Subject to availability


TWO-WHEELED VEHICLES (Motorcycles)

Free / Lyon €5
(reservation required)

Maximum 2 parking spaces per match*

Reserved for two-wheeled motorcycles
(three-wheeled authorized if width < 1.2m)

Available on all venues

*Subject to availability

Rules about parking

  • Except for Lyon*, the parking offer will be available exclusively on the website tickets.rugbyworldcup.com
  • No exchange or refund will be possible
  • The resale of your parking space will not be authorized
  • Your parking space is for single use only. Any exit of the parking lot will be final

Security measures

Security guards will be deployed to facilitate your arrival and departure in the parking lots, and to allow you to have the best experience around Rugby World Cup 2023 venues.

However, France 2023 cannot be held responsible for any theft or damage inside the parking areas.

*Special case of the parking lots in Lyon?

The parking spaces for OL Stadium in Lyon will be exclusively sold by Olympique Lyonnais for Rugby World Cup 2023. When you will select your match in the match list, you will be automatically redirected to the dedicated website. You will then be asked to create a specific customer account on this dedicated webiste https://france2023.ol.fr/en.

To buy your parking spaces in Lyon, you can also click here.

Steps to book your parking space

From your browser, access the official 2023 Rugby World Cup ticketing service

Enter your login details
If you are already logged in, go to the next step
If you want to buy ACCESSIBILITY parking, log you in with the account you used to book your tickets

Click on the menu "PARKING BOOKING"

Click on PARKINGS LIST

Choose the match of your choice

Choose the type of parking you want

Complete your booking by registering your payment or finalizing your order
